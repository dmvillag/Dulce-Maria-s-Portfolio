ABOUT ME

Cultural web designer and front-end developer blending tradition and innovation to craft immersive digital experiences. I create emotionally rich, visually dynamic websites that highlight Latin American stories through intuitive code and thoughtful design.

With curiosity, empathy, and connection at the core of my process, I celebrate cultural identity and spark meaningful engagement through design that is accessible, bilingual, and proudly rooted in storytelling.

CASE STUDIES

Refactoring Wunderlust Site with Semantic HTML

Wunderlust began as a journey into structure, transforming cluttered code into clean, semantic HTML. It wasn’t just about tags; it was about telling a story machines and humans could both understand. This refactor brought clarity, accessibility, and purpose to the travel brand’s online presence. It was the first step in understanding how small changes in code can elevate user experience and honor the intention behind every word and image.

Personal Portfolio with Interactive Features

My portfolio project was a playful challenge to merge personality with function. Through interactive features like light/dark mode, a guessing game, and a photo gallery, I crafted a digital space that reflects who I am as a designer and developer. It’s not just a showcase; it’s an experience built to surprise and delight. Each line of code became a way to invite users into my creative process and evolving story.

“El Pueblito Feliz”: A Cultural Exploration of Moroleón

El Pueblito Feliz is more than a website; it’s a celebration of Moroleón, my hometown. I designed this project to highlight traditional dishes, vibrant local shops, and Latin American cinema, all through a bilingual, user-friendly platform. Blending nostalgia with digital design, the site honors identity and community while inviting cultural curiosity. It became the heart of my portfolio, a reminder of where I come from and who I design for.
